Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

CPATSBI Hub ("we", "this site") provides free browser-based tools, including a WhatsApp message formatter and a PDF-to-PNG splitter. This policy explains what data is and isn't collected when you use this site.

Tool data — processed locally, not uploaded

The text you paste into the WhatsApp Formatter or Topper Congrats Builder, and any PDF file you drop into the PDF Splitter, are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing you type, paste, or upload is sent to our servers — we don't have a server component that receives this data at all.

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or generally through www.aboutads.info.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Analytics

We may use standard web analytics (such as Google Analytics, if enabled) to understand aggregate traffic patterns — for example, how many people visit the site and which pages they view. This data is anonymized and not linked to any content you enter into the tools.

Cookies

This site may use cookies placed by third-party services (such as Google AdSense) for advertising purposes. We do not set our own tracking cookies. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Third-party links

This site may link to external resources (such as Google's ad settings). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
